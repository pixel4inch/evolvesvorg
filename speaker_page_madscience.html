SESSION 1

Innovation Through Forward Inventions

The world is heading towards convergence of different technologies to develop solutions that can serve specific requirements and bring more value to the investment. Developments geared towards making solutions more robust and capable of performing in difficult conditions and scenarios will especially find robust demand in coming years. A new breed of technology solutions will emerge in the immediate future, which will amalgamate businesses, government, and institutions with technology.

This session will help attendees

  • Learn about new technologies that would be possible game changers in future
  • Gain insights on advanced developments in materials, technologies and devices in areas such as science, electronics, medicine, and biomaterials among others.
  • Learn about scientists with thoughts and visions ahead of their time
  • Identify and coordinate with well-known subject matter experts
  • Understand issues related to novel inventions and innovations
  • Share information and cross-pollinate ideas on technology of the future

Dr. Vanessa Shannon

Director of Mental Performance for the Cincinnati Reds

Vanessa Shannon has been the Director of Mental Performance for the University of Louisville Athletic Department and Norton Sports Health since October 2015. Dr. Shannon holds a PhD in Kinesiology, Recreation, and Sports Studies with a specialization in Sport Psychology and concentration in Counseling (University of Tennessee) a MS in Kinesiology with an emphasis in Exercise Psychology (Kansas State University) and BAs in Health and Human Performance and Psychology from Rice University.

SESSION 3

Strangest Robotic Inventions that will Make You Think the Future is Now

Once limited to science fiction, and gradually transcending to industrial applications, robots have evolved to play an increasingly important role in every sector led by the convergence of robotics with cognitive science, artificial intelligence (AI), and information technology. The ample opportunities for implementation of robotics in everyday life is leading researchers to focus on developing robots for use in unconventional environments. There is yet still a need for significant advancements in the fields of computer vision, automated reasoning, motion planning, and language processing, to transform the concept of a personal robot into a commercial reality.

This session will help attendees

  • Discover emerging robotic technologies and get an opportunity to learn about some of the fun inventions that give a sneak peek into our future.
  • Meet industry experts and leaders in robotics from around the world and learn from their commercial robotics development experiences and expertise.
  • Broaden general understanding and perspectives of robotics
  • Share insights, discuss business opportunities and partnerships with industry peers.

This session will help attendees

  • Learn about convergence of technologies that hold the potential to revolutionize military training
  • Opportunity to network with technology companies and industry experts and share insights
