SESSION 1

Re-Invention of the Food System. How Do We Achieve Scale & Acceleration?

Recent shocks to the food system caused by the pandemic, war and climate change has underlined the dire need to re-invent agriculture, supply chain systems, food security and food quality. How effective have we been at integrating digital technology into the farm production system? Are governments and regulators playing their role in promoting use of technologies that have a positive impact on the quality?

This session will help attendees

  • Gain insights into leading agritech solutions & innovations; understand the value of farm-to-fork transparency; and standardization of data and related tools.
  • Create conversations, inspire fellow participants with new thinking and bridge the gap between research and application.
  • Learn better ways to accelerate the deployment of technology solutions and research
  • Understand the importance of collaboration in driving the transformation.
  • Meet, interact and collaborate with ecosystem players such as farmers, producers, manufacturers, retailers, technology providers and financial investors.

SESSION 2

Incentivizing Regenerative Agriculture. Are Farmers Put at the Center of Climate Action?

The regenerative agriculture movement is fairly new and has long way to go before it can be adopted as an alternative to current conventional, large-scale agriculture. There remains a lot of inadequacies in the framework and standards supporting this concept.

This session will help attendees

  • Understand the pros and cons of regenerative agriculture
  • Change your perspectives around business models and practices to follow.
  • Identify what’s needed to fully de-risk barriers to implementation.
  • Gain insights into the framework and standards needed to ensure that the concept meets climate, nature and societal goals and achieve expected outcomes.

SESSION 3

Optimization & Value Creation Through Smart Control of Agriculture Supply-Chain

The importance of integrating intelligence into the notoriously complex agriculture supply chain can never be underrated. Digital and analytics technologies are the way forward for constructing a high-performance, resilient and flexible supply chain. But are companies handling digitalization of supply chain the right way? Do they have the right planning in place to be able to access vast amounts of data & analyze them in near real-time to accurately forecast future demand or changes in the supply chain?

This session will help attendees

  • Understand and fully appreciate how hyperledger blockchain can be leveraged to improve transparency and traceability in the supply chain
  • Learn how AI can be adroitly used to predict & mitigate the impact of weather conditions by dynamically recalibrating inventory stocking decisions
  • Gain insights into how IoT sensors can be smartly deployed to boost warehouse efficiency
  • Develop a framework & apply globally applicable benchmarks to measure progress of efforts taken in this direction

SESSION 4

Drones for Agriculture”. Are We Ready for Them?

Although we have heard about the revolutionizing impact of drones in industrial, military and consumer industries, their use in agriculture is just beginning to take flight. Drones promise to bring in a huge change in enhancing the efficiency of agricultural operations. An emerging field, now is the right time to brainstorm various facets of drone use in agriculture; and share knowledge among stakeholders about the opportunities and challenges in store for this high impact technology of the future.

This session will help attendees

  • Shed light on the many Government initiatives set into motion for promoting use of drone for agriculture
  • Learn & identify the various application opportunities for drones in agriculture
  • Gain insights & explore solutions to overcome the challenges & limitations faced during practical deployment
  • Brainstorm the possibilities of making drone technology easy accessible to farmers
  • Learn various ways to measure ROI on drone deployment projects
  • Sharpen your ability to fully leverage drones as a service (DaaS) to achieve 12:1 ROI on investments
