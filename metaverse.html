WHAT'S TRENDING


Metaverse is the latest buzzword capturing the imagination of the tech industry and holds significant potential in creating tremendous growth opportunities for marketers by allowing them to deliver advanced immersive and interactive digital experiences to their customers. The metaverse market is propelled by rising applications associated with diverse domains like content creation, gaming, social interaction, virtual shopping and training or learning. The metaverse is poised to find extensive uptake across diverse industries over the coming years, indicating an impressive growth potential. The sheer size of the digital economy and its shift towards the metaverse are slated to create new growth avenues for the market. The trend is prompting leading companies like Nike, Wal-Mart, Gap, Hulu, Verizon, Atari and Adidas to join the bandwagon for brand recognition and expansion of the customer base. The market is bound to benefit from rising attention on metaverse platforms centered on communication and social interaction.


Metaverse technology is offering successful opportunities for consumers and companies operating in the fitness and gaming industries. However, other verticals can also create valuable products and user experiences. For instance, the medical and healthcare fields have a significant opportunity to provide considerably improved telemedicine experiences and enhanced access to healthcare by overcoming geographical barriers. Metaverse enables the creation of a digital twin of a whole human body or a body organ in a 3D format, which allows for performing surgeries, and treating and diagnosing diseases. Metaverse implementations have already been successful in medical training and education. Moreover, the entertainment and publishing industries also have opportunities to offer unique immersive experiences in the metaverse. The Industry is poised to witness notable expansion in the coming years owing to rising popularity of platforms, strong interest and adoption across industries and technological advancements.


Metaverse is taking shape with immersive gaming worlds taking a lead on how users experience the virtual reality using their virtual identities and how they socially interact, tour, engage in pleasure activities and conduct business within the digital world using either real or cryptocurrency. Second Life, Fortnite, Roblox and Decentraland are some of the most popular games that indulged digital audience in concerts, purchases of plots, shopping for luxury goods and services, gambling and touring art galleries. Scientific thrillers are fuelling the conversations by lending imagination to the concept of metaverse on the celluloid. Kicked up by the upping market interest these companies have started investing in developmental efforts of Metaverse. Buoyed by the enthusiasm across board, technology companies are leveraging on the popularity of social media to devise an additional universe gamifying concepts of living and interacting using augmented reality in virtual space.

Metaverse is currently in a development stage and technologies are being deployed to add new physical dimensions to the digital interaction space featuring smells, touch, temperature, sounds and places to render the user experience real and active while ensuring safety and control over environments. Companies are thoroughly modelling security scenarios to make the metaverse digitally safe, controlled and responsible without privacy breaches. 5G mobility, rising popularity of trackable digital goods through non fungible tokens (NFTs) and other nascent digital technologies are strapping up to provide a comprehensive technology and financial base for creating metaverse. Device independence, platform interoperability, minimal/non obtrusive physical specs of VR tools, coupled with advanced augmentation and cost economy ensure seamless avatar mobility across metaverse’s multiple virtual interactions.

The metaverse market is dominated by North America buoyed by strong focus of various start-ups to set up metaverse platforms and solutions to commercialize and push the concept. These efforts are anticipated to create attractive revenue generation opportunities for the market. Growth is poised to be augmented by high popularity of gaming along with the presence of leading player such as Nvidia Corporation, The Sandbox, and Epic Games, Inc. These players are embarking on ambitious efforts to incorporate metaverse into advanced games. In a notable development, Nvidia Corporation has joined hands with Adobe and Blender towards NVIDIA Omniverse, a simulation platform intended to cover millions of users. The metaverse market in Asia-Pacific is predicted to exhibit an impressive growth over the coming years. The regional market is slated to receive a strong contribution from ongoing efforts by leading players to promote the concept. Virtual metaverse marketplaces along with gaming platforms are anticipated to deliver engaging experience and help users in enjoying live activities supported by third-party apps.

A number of high impact technologies have helped make the metaverse dream a reality. A few of the noteworthy enabling technologies are discussed below.


  • AR/VR/MR/XR - AR and VR especially stand out for their instrumental role in powering the future metaverse. Being major Immersive technologies that work by overlaying a digital layer of experience over daily reality, AR and VR have revolutionized the way we interact and experience the virtual world. Rapid advancements and improvements have helped create immersive alternate reality that today is helping form the foundation for the metaverse. Defined as a digital representation of oneself, the use of avatar will revolutionize the world of communication, not just in the social media space but also in the field of enterprise collaboration and communication. Meta’s business agenda behind the purchase of Oculus Rift is to create and monetize the use of electronic avatars. Fruition of the technology’s promised capabilities will result in over 1 billion people spending an increasing portion of their time wearing, managing and controlling their electronic avatars. VR is therefore forecast to emerge as a multidisciplinary communication tool in social networking, the ad industry, and in the telecommunication industry. In the emerging field of metaverse, VR headsets and AR glasses will provide the ability to virtually live in this parallel alternate reality universe.
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) - Publishers always want their users to return and continue investing, while also growing with a particular environment. Tools like AI that gather audience information for learning and understanding the audience wll be largely responsible for the Metaverse’s growth in the next frontier of the gaming industry. AI has already become an inextricable part of daily work and living and assists with the testing, checking, coding and even automatic generation of whole story segments. With more people becoming creators of digital content, it is expected that AI will assume the role of a creative aide which will work with human creators and automate repetitive, difficult, or boring tasks which form a part of any creative process. AI systems learn from previous patterns and examples seen in a Metaverse, subsequently using this learnt information in assisting with the newer creative processes. Additionally, AI has a significant role to play in the Metaverse because AI systems in future will identify and know users, thus, shaping their individual Metaverse-related experiences accordingly.
  • Digital Twins – This technology will help people in moving between virtual and real spaces. Digital twins are the building blocks of metaverse and use intelligent edge and intelligent cloud to create digital models running from simple assets to complex environments. Metaverse is a network of digital spaces used in virtual-reality worlds and video-game environments. Digital twins can be seen as clones of real-world systems and objects and can be used to develop, evolve or design our cities.
  • Edge Computing & 5G - Edge computing is rapidly emerging as a technology innovation that can be widely adopted across industries. Some of key benefits of using intelligent edge include capability for quick decision making and low latency. Edge meets local computing requirements as data is processed in micro-data centers. On-site data processing helps to eliminate latency and address network and connectivity challenges. Every edge computing unit would have dedicated storage, computer and network system. When coupled with 5G, there is strong potential to considerably lower network latency. Edge computing and 5G would support situational awareness along with data analysis for on-spot decision making. Cognitive intelligence and autonomous decision-making reduce processing as well as decision making latency. The approach allows faster and almost real-time decisions. The evolution of edge analytics and 5G beyond conventional descriptive analytics is expected to provide users with the ability to predict, take appropriate actions, and learn from past behavioral patterns for enhanced decisions. The availability and processing of more data delivered by 5G is anticipated to help edge analytics in generating meaningful insights, improving the decision making process and supporting applications related to cognitive intelligence. Edge computing along with 5G will therefore enable users to engage meaningfully in the metaverse and enjoy a truly immersive experience.
  • Blockchain - As the stride towards metaverse intensifies, blockchains are slated to don an integral role in ascribing authenticity to electronic transactions, both for fungible and non-fungible tokens. NFTs are emerging as integral to gaming, especially for monetizing user-created content and for secure transactions across the platform. Cryptocurrencies that possess standard valuation are substitutable for each other while non-fungible tokens (NFTs) are exclusive digital assets created by individuals for trading across platforms with unique valuation and cannot be interchanged in terms of value. With the popularity of NFTs surging, the road ahead for blockchain metaverse is brimming with possibilities riding on the popularity of metaverse rush across the globe. NFTs range from art works, GIFs, images, music, videos to any other digital content that commands value. The uniqueness of this asset class is akin to copyrights where the ownership is vested in individuals who create and assign value independently. NFTs across blockchain platforms are emerging as the foundation for creating virtual worlds that are popularizing play to earn concept. In other words, a player can earn revenue by selling the characters or tokens earned through gaming. As such, gamers are touted to be the early adopters of metaverse owing to their comfort levels in navigating within the virtual space and ability to communicate across the gaming environment. Another key tenet in metaverse is the portability of 3D avatars and digital assets across the interoperating platforms. Blockchain enables the digital movement and tracking of resources and NFTs owned by individuals across the metaverse.

An effervescent technology concept, the metaverse market is bubbling with activity ranging from venture capital investments, mushrooming of new start-ups, enterprise strategies to move business models to the metaverse, development of new, innovative metaverse compatible product and services, among others. It is important for business leaders to keep in touch with their markets and be abreast of all new emerging trends. Discussed below are few trends that capture the pulse of the market.


  • 5G to Fastrack Metaverse Initiatives - 5G is deemed to be the future of the mobile industry, bringing forth leading edge technologies and innovations that provide more effective communication. A big benefit associated with 5G will be a decrease in latency that enables faster and high speed data transmission. . 5G network technologies that will be deployed between 2020 and 2030 will offer features such as stable connections, zero-distance connectivity for machines connected to people, and uploads and downloads at extremely fast speeds, stated to be 10-100 times the speed gains over 4G networks. While public metaverse is yet to be deployed and may take several more years, the role of 5G network technology and hyperscaler infrastructure will be significant in enabling the same. Metaverse technology in its basic form, namely integration and overlay of physical and digital twins, is already in existence and use to resolve several issues such as how to cover city using 5G coverage that can support public metaverse infrastructure. In the metaverse space, there are numerous features and requirements that go farther than pure connectivity specifications, for which 5G network technology proves beneficial. Several wireless connectivity technologies are availability today for enabling wireless connectivity. However, all of them suffer from some drawbacks. While Bluetooth lacks reliability, range and rate, Wi-Fi has issues related to congestion and high latencies when numerous XR devices are simultaneously connected. In contract, 5G technology offers range, rate, reliability and latency needed for metaverse. Currently, average DL/UL data rates of 5G are around 2000Mbps/30Mbps per user.
  • Marketers to Enter the Metaverse in a Big Way - The advent of the internet and technological advancements in mobile devices resulted in a shift in marketing strategies from magazines and billboards to SEO and social media. The metaverse would also impact the marketing activities of brands in a similar manner. Marketers would be required to direct their focus on adapting to emerging technologies at a more rapid pace. They would need to find where the consumers could be found or located in the metaverse. Brands must rethink on their narratives and build and present them in a world of three dimensions. Any brand that wants to stay relevant in the market would need to adopt all the changes that metaverse brings in. The metaverse will also affect the way brands implement their SEO efforts. One of the important aspects of metaverse is the critical role played by image processing. Everything including visual search, virtual reality, and augmented reality relies on image processing. Deep content, which is the core factor of any SEO Strategy, must serve the needs of the user most efficiently in order to perform well in organic search traffic and excel in SEO rankings. Images represent the most effective form of any content. For any brand, a better understanding of the content through the optimal quality of images is key to becoming a bigger influencer. Games are the major means through which brands are entering into the virtual space, where they could natively insert themselves into the world of experiences, rather than interrupting them.
  • Manufacturing in the Metaverse to Hit Prime Time - The metaverse is anticipated to wield a major impact on manufacturing in the future. While the approach of digital-first is driven by actions and preferences of the consumer, in the metaverse, consumers would become the creators and designers. They would create new demand and also change how products are made. Designing of production processes would become rapid in the metaverse helped by better virtual spaces that simulate production processes of facility. Business owners would be able to witness quickly, how moving specific assets around could impact production. Car manufacturers have already started using it for simulating their manufacturing facilities and were able to plan production processes more accurately. BMW is an example. Real physics can thus be simulated and ways for making work safer and more efficient can be identified, without actually having to stop or slow down production for achieving the same results achieved using physical changes or testing. The general public would be given the ability to simulate 3D prototypes of products which would render more people to create, just like how the YouTube channel has numerous content creators. Creators and designers will no longer be required to go through intermediaries for getting their creations to manufacturers and this will help increase number of designs exponentially. Product design will also become increasingly collaborative and rapid due to availability of specialized tools.
  • Future of Work Will Shift from WFH to WFM - The future of work is remote and online communication. Metaverse will go a long way in reshaping the digital economy by opening up the possibility of the ‘infinite office’. Emerging technologies have transformed traditional working. Virtual reality will be harnessed not only for gaming and entertainment but also for creating a shared virtual space. Digital companies are racing towards this digital frontier that will transform the future of the workplace and work itself. The Horizon Workrooms from Facebook is a VR avatar of virtual meeting space. Be it Roblox, Minecraft, Fortnite, or the other metaverse players, several billion users are being introduced to a new virtual office experience from home. Each tech giant is building its own metaverse. The Infinite office will revolutionize the work-from-home concept. Users can develop and share collaborative projects, play games, and socialize. They can attend concerts, shop, purchase houses, and play video games. While AR and VR technologies have been used by astronauts, soldiers, and surgeons, other industries like customer service, retail, and logistics are on the threshold of employing them. With metaverse products, people can be more productive by reducing duplication of effort and promoting collaborative working. Metaverse will open up new vistas of work and interaction. Freelancing and remote working will become easier with virtual options. Management can monitor employees more effectively.
