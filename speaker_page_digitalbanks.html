SESSION 1

Re-Invention of the Food System. How Do We Achieve Scale & Acceleration?

Recent shocks to the food system caused by the pandemic, war and climate change has underlined the dire need to re-invent agriculture, supply chain systems, food security and food quality. How effective have we been at integrating digital technology into the farm production system? Are governments and regulators playing their role in promoting use of technologies that have a positive impact on the quality?

This session will help attendees

  • Gain insights into leading agritech solutions & innovations; understand the value of farm-to-fork transparency; and standardization of data and related tools.
  • Create conversations, inspire fellow participants with new thinking and bridge the gap between research and application.
  • Learn better ways to accelerate the deployment of technology solutions and research
  • Understand the importance of collaboration in driving the transformation.
  • Meet, interact and collaborate with ecosystem players such as farmers, producers, manufacturers, retailers, technology providers and financial investors.

SESSION 2

How Will Climate Risk Regulations Impact Banking Sector?

The banking industry is expected to witness implementation of Climate Risk regulations. How are banking institutions preparing for impending climate-related regulations?

This session will help attendees

  • Understand the various climate related risks for the banks
  • Get insights into how climate risk regulations will impact the banking sector
  • Learn how bankers can be incentivized to create and implement climate risk related policies and reports

SESSION 3

Digital Transformation and the Importance of Ethics in Digital Banking

The rapid pace of technology developments is impacting companies, employees and customers. However, most often the ethical aspects of such development are often not considered. How important are ethics in digital banking?

This session will help attendees

  • Understand the most ethical issues facing the digital banking industry
  • Get insights into ways digital ethics can be aligned with corporate goals and values
  • Learn how good digital ethical practices can be incorporated into existing processes and policies
  • Understand how to build digital ethics governance structure in banks

SESSION 4

How Important Is The Role of AI & Data Science in Banking Sector?

The role of AI and data science continues to grow in the banking, finance and insurance sectors. Rising demand for secure transactions and minimal customer services interactions from both financial institutions and consumers are driving significant of cutting-edge technologies. How will AI and data science shape the future of banking industry?

This session will help attendees

  • Understand the ways in which AI can shape digital and omnichannel experiences
  • Get insights into ways AI can be implemented beyond the niche applications
  • Learn how AI and data tools can support personalized client relationship
  • Understand why chatbots have become the must-have in customer service
